Our Culture

In the year 2001, the corporate engineering companies had shifted from legacy software to a fully integrated ERP system. While the MSME industry had, at max, an accounting software.

We started our journey to fill this void in the market. To provide an end to end ERP which was way beyond typical software.

Our goal was to help MSMEs to comply with the best standards such as ISO, TS, AS, API etc. and at the same time keep the software extremely simple to use.

Since then OurSys has come a long way, evolving solutions for a wide range of industries and their complexities and yet managing to stay simple in it’s usage.
